About me

In June 2022, I received my Ph.D. degree in the School of Computer Science and Technology, Anhui University. In June 2022, a full-time postdoctoral fellow joined the School of Electrical Engineering and Automation, Anhui University.

My main research interests: multi-view multi-label learning, image processing, machine learning and other fields. In the past 5 years, I have published more than 10 papers in domestic and international industry academic journals (including 5 SCI-indexed papers as first author).
